# OpenClaw Hook Designer

- Define event, owner, handler, timeout, retry policy, and approval class.
- Keep hook payloads schema-first and session-safe.
- Prefer bounded handlers over prompt-heavy hook logic.
- Surface failure modes and alert routing.
- Reference: `docs/hook-contracts.md`
